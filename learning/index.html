How this works.

Alpha teaches you how to pronounce Hanyu Pinyin translated into the Roman alphabet. If you want to learn Mandarin Chinese, learning Pinyin is the first step.

The learning section provides descriptions of the sounds and how to pronounce them.

Navigate through the different slides with the buttons below and when you're ready to take the test, click the button matching the section you covered.

ShengMu Letters

Sheng Mu is the initial consonant of a Chinese syllable.

YunMu Letters

Yun Mu is the vowel portion of the phonetic alphabet. It is combined with Sheng Mu to make up a complete sound.

ZhengTi Letters

Zheng Ti is the whole syllable.
